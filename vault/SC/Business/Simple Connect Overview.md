# Simple Connect — Business Overview

## Mission
Done-for-you business systems for service companies. We install and manage website, booking, automation, invoicing, and payments so clients can focus on the work.

## Target Market
- Home service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, landscaping, cleaning)
- Trade shops
- Local service companies

## Core Value Proposition
- AI-powered CRM and automation
- Website + booking system
- Lead follow-up automation (SMS, email, voice)
- Invoicing and payment collection
- Client communication on autopilot

## Product Tiers
| Tier | Focus |
|---|---|
| Starter | Website + booking |
| Growth | Full lead follow-up (SMS, email, voice — 5 touches/7 days) |
| Pro | Full platform + AI automation + SMS conversation handling |

## Website
- URL: https://www.simpleconnect2.com
- Tech: React SPA hosted on VPS
- Booking: Native calendar modal → posts to OS API
- Chat widget: LeadConnector

## OS Platform
- URL: https://os.simpleconnect2.com
- Login: admin credentials in `.env.sc`
- Modules: CRM, Inbox, Appointments, Campaigns, Automations, Invoices, Projects, Site Studio, Reports
