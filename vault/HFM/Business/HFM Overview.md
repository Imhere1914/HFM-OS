# Holistic Functional Care — Business Overview

## About
Holistic Functional Care (HFM) is a functional medicine / holistic health practice. The AI OS platform manages patient scheduling, communications, CRM, and practice operations.

## OS Platform
- **URL:** https://os.holisticfunctionalcare.com
- **Website:** https://www.holisticfunctionalcare.com
- **Brand:** `hfm`
- **GitHub:** https://github.com/Imhere1914/HFM-OS

## Platform Modules in Use
- **Contacts** — Patient CRM
- **Appointments** — Patient scheduling
- **Conversations** — Inbox for patient communications
- **Campaigns** — Patient outreach and newsletters
- **Automations** — Appointment reminders, follow-ups
- **Invoices** — Patient billing
- **Site Studio** — Website content management
- **Scheduling** — Multi-calendar appointment booking
